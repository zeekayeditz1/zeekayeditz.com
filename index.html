About Me

Crafting Cinematic Stories Through Motion

I'm Zahid, a motion designer and video editor operating under the brand ZeeKay Editz. I specialize in transforming raw footage into visually stunning content — from cinematic video edits and motion graphics to custom After Effects animations and full-scale YouTube productions.

With a deep passion for the craft and a keen eye for detail, I work closely with content creators, brands, and agencies to bring creative visions to life. Based in Pakistan — working with clients worldwide.
